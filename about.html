About Ari Global Industries

Engineering global industrial solutions with precision, reliability, and scale.

Who We Are

Ari Global Industries is a globally oriented industrial solutions and trade company specializing in the supply of machinery, industrial materials, engineered systems, and customized sourcing solutions across international markets.

The company operates at the intersection of global demand and manufacturing capability, delivering solutions that are not limited to products alone, but extend to complete coordination, technical alignment, and execution across supply chains.

With a strong foundation in sourcing, engineering understanding, and logistics management, Ari Global Industries ensures that every requirement is met with precision, consistency, and efficiency.

Leadership

Founded by Arjun Sharma, Ari Global Industries was established with a clear objective, that is to streamline global trade by connecting dependable manufacturing ecosystems with evolving international requirements.

From the outset, the vision has been to go beyond conventional trading models by integrating sourcing expertise, operational discipline, and technical coordination into a single, efficient framework.

Under focused leadership, the company continues to expand its global footprint while maintaining a strong commitment to reliability, execution excellence, and long-term value creation.

“Our objective is not limited to supplying products. We aim to deliver structured industrial solutions that ensure consistency, efficiency, and long-term operational value.”

Customized Sourcing Solutions

Ari Global Industries offers flexible sourcing solutions tailored to specific client requirements. Whether it involves specialized machinery, unique materials, or niche industrial components, the company leverages an extensive supplier network to identify, procure, and deliver with precision.

This capability allows the organization to function as a comprehensive sourcing and execution platform rather than a conventional trading entity.

Industrial Expertise & Turnkey Solutions

Ari Global Industries extends beyond conventional supply by offering complete industrial solutions, including turnkey manufacturing plants, production systems, and engineered process lines.

Our capabilities enable us to support clients from initial concept and machinery sourcing to installation coordination and operational readiness, ensuring every project is delivered as a structured and functional solution.

Biscuit Manufacturing Plant Solutions

Complete solutions for biscuit production including fully automatic and semi-automatic plants, travelling ovens (electric, diesel, or gas-fired), dough mixers, cream mixers, laminators, cooling conveyors, and high-speed packing systems.

We also provide moulding systems, cutting dies, tray forming machines, and integrated conveyor lines ensuring smooth and efficient production flow.

Turnkey Industrial Manufacturing Plants

End-to-end solutions for various industries including PET bottle manufacturing plants, cap production systems, juice processing lines, sheet extrusion plants (HIPS, PP, PET), and packaging systems.

Additional solutions include thermoforming machines, shrink tunnels, inspection systems, and fully integrated production setups.

Engineering, Automation & Infrastructure

Comprehensive industrial support including engineering design, machinery sourcing, installation coordination, automation integration, and plant modernization.

Our approach ensures every system is aligned for efficiency, scalability, and long-term operational performance.
