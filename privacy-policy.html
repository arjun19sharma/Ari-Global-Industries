Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here’s how we handle your information.

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Ari Global Industries (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any information you share with us while visiting our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information, even though our website is primarily informational and does not host forms or interactive user accounts.

By accessing or using our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Since our website is purely front-end, we do not actively collect personal information from visitors.

However, the following may occur:

  • Automatically Collected Data: Your browser may send standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited for analytical purposes.
  • Optional Contact Information: If you contact us directly via email, phone, or other channels, the information you provide (name, email, phone, message) will be used only for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

3. How We Use Information

Information collected is used only to:

  • Respond to inquiries or requests submitted to us directly
  • Improve the content, usability, and functionality of our website
  • Analyze general website traffic trends for operational improvements
We do not use personal information for marketing or automated profiling.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use basic cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience, such as remembering language preferences or analyzing visitor trends.

You may disable cookies at any time via your browser settings; however, some features may not function optimally if cookies are disabled.

We do not use cookies to track individuals across other websites for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, tools, or services (for example, social media links). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

6. Data Security

While our website does not store user accounts or sensitive personal data, we implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any information we may receive from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements.
Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
